Strategic Role Choice Questions

Detailed reference for K-12 educator workshop - Full question text for potential integration with decision tree SVG

Complete Decision Framework

0

To AI or Not to AI?

Should AI be involved at all?

  • • Is this a foundational skill?
  • • Do students need independent practice?
  • • Learning vs. producing?
→ If learning core skills: Consider no AI first
→ If building foundation: Stay human-only
→ If joy of doing/hobbies/creative: Maybe no AI
1

Objectives & Evaluation

What are the learning goals?

  • • Skill practice vs. polished output?
  • • Assessment focus?
  • • Success metrics?
→ Skill-building focus: Human-Led roles (Strategist/Implementer)
→ Production focus: Consider AI-Led roles
2

Resources & Support

What tools and knowledge exist?

  • • AI access (free/paid)?
  • • Experience level?
  • • Available help?
→ Limited resources: Strategist/Implementer
→ Ample resources: Can consider Curator/Orchestrator
3

Constraints & Obligations

Time, format, and policy limits?

  • • Class time?
  • • Format requirements?
  • • School AI policies?
→ Tight deadlines: Curator/Orchestrator
→ Flexible time: Can focus on Strategist/Implementer for learning
4

Tool-Task Fit

Does AI match the learning need?

  • • AI strength area?
  • • Enhances learning?
  • • Preserves thinking?
→ Match role to human judgment needs
→ Discovery phase: Consider human control
→ Delivery phase: Can delegate more to AI

Role Mapping Summary

Human-Led + Discovery = STRATEGIST: You explore, AI assists with research

Human-Led + Delivery = IMPLEMENTER: You build, AI assists with execution

AI-Led + Discovery = CURATOR: AI gathers broadly, you evaluate and select

AI-Led + Delivery = ORCHESTRATOR: AI produces, you refine and coordinate
