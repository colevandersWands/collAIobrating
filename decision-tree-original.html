Strategic Role Choice: Detailed Decision Framework

1

Objectives & Evaluation

What are the learning goals? What does success look like?

• Is the goal to practice a specific skill or produce a polished output?
• What competencies are being assessed?
• How will you measure student understanding?
→ If skill-building is key: lean toward Human-Led roles
2

Resources & Support

What tools are available? What prior knowledge exists?

• What AI tools can students access (free vs. paid)?
• What's their experience level with both the subject and AI?
• Who can help if they get stuck?
→ Limited resources or experience: start with Strategist or Implementer
3

Constraints & Obligations

Time limits? Assessment requirements? Privacy concerns?

• How much class time is available?
• Are there specific format or citation requirements?
• What are the school's AI use policies?
→ Tight deadlines: consider Curator or Orchestrator for efficiency
4

Tool-Task Fit

Is AI appropriate here? Which role best supports learning?

• Does AI excel at this type of task?
• Will AI use enhance or bypass learning?
• How can we preserve critical thinking?
→ Match role to where human judgment adds most value
